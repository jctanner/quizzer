Express the following compound propositions in English using the following definitions:

p: I am going to a movie tonight.
q: I am going to the party tonight.

(a) ¬p

I am not going to a movie tonight.

(b)p ∧ q

I am going to a movie and the party tonight.

(c) p ∧ ¬q

I am going to a movie and not the party tonight.

(d) ¬p ∨ ¬q

I am either not going to a movie or not going to the party (or neither) tonight.

(e) ¬(p ∧ q)

It is not true that I am going to a movie and the party tonight.
